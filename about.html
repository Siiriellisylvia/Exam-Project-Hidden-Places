HELLO, NICE TO SEE YOU HERE!

WHAT?

This is a local initiative to showcase an often overlooked district of Barcelona - Poblenou. The area is a mix and match of industrial and traditional. It is home to many sustainable initiatives, creative spaces and much more. It is always changing, always fun.

WHO?

I'm Siiri, and I have lived in the area for many years. It does not stop surprising me! I could spend days just wandering around, and often do. I have listed some of my favourite places on this website.

WHY?

You're always guaranteed to find something interesting, it is a hidden treasure full of creativity and life in every corner. Often some of the most abandoned looking places turn out to be pop-up second hand markets, or pole dancing studios.

You never know what you'll find.

And that's the beauty of it!
