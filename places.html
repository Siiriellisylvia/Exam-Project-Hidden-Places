CASA TAOS

Interesting catchy stuff here

From the outside you can't really tell what hides inside - but peek in and be welcomed with open arms!

They are happy to tell you about the concept, they're very proud of it, as they should! The cafe is zero waste, and Taste the daily menu and check out the locally made ceramics hiding in the corner! You can also have a look at the green coworking space behind the glass, it's for members only but you can get a day pass!
